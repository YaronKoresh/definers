# Video Workflows

Definers exposes video composition, lyric-video, visualizer, and rendering workflows through both modules and launchers.

## Start Here

- `definers.video`
- `definers.ui.music_video_service`
- focused launcher surfaces such as `video-composer`, `video-lyrics`, and `video-visualizer`

## Best Fit

- music visualizers
- lyric videos
- composed video surfaces
- frame-level feature extraction and reconstruction

Use this capability when the output is a rendered video artifact, not just extracted media features.
